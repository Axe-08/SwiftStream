The Landscape of ASR Architectures

  • RNN-Transducer: The classic streaming standard. Good performance but computationally heavy and can struggle with long-range context. Ref: Graves, 2012
  • Attention Models (Whisper): Extremely accurate by using the full audio context, but inherently non-streaming, resulting in high latency. Ref: Radford et al., 2023
  • Conformer: The previous state-of-the-art, blending convolutions and self-attention. Highly accurate but its O(n²) complexity is a bottleneck. Ref: Gulati et al., 2020
  • State-Space Models (Mamba): The new challenger. Linear-time O(n) complexity allows for modeling extremely long sequences efficiently, making it ideal for streaming. Ref: Gu & Dao, 2023

Why Mamba Wins for Streaming

It's all about Computational Complexity.

Transformer/Conformer's O(n²) complexity means processing time explodes as audio length increases. Mamba's linear O(n) complexity is vastly more efficient and scalable.

Phase 1: The Baseline MVP

Why adapt a non-streaming model first?

  • 1. High-Quality Benchmark: Whisper provides state-of-the-art accuracy, creating a powerful performance target to beat.
  • 2. De-Risk the Project: We can solve system-level challenges (API, containerization, deployment) without the initial complexity of a custom model.
  • 3. Justify the Need: It will provide hard data on the limitations of adapting non-streaming models, powerfully justifying our final architecture. Methodology based on: Gandhi et al., 2023 (Distil-Whisper)
